# 🤖💬 CHEMVENTUR v118 QUICKSTART GUIDE

## Get Started with AI Players & Chat in 2 Minutes! ⚡

---

## 🚀 INSTANT START (3 Steps!)

### **Step 1: Open the Game** 🎮
- Open `index.html` in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
- You'll see the main game screen

### **Step 2: Connect to Multiplayer** 🌐
- Click the **"🌐 CONNECT TO MULTIPLAYER"** button in the right panel
- Wait a few seconds for connection
- You'll see **"✅ CONNECTED!"**

### **Step 3: AI & Chat Auto-Start!** 🤖💬
- **AI Bots** spawn automatically (you'll see them moving!)
- **Chat window** appears in bottom-right corner
- **You're ready to play!** 🎉

---

## 🤖 USING AI PLAYERS

### **What You'll See:**
1. **📦 Collector Bot (Cyan)** - Collecting atoms carefully
2. **🔬 Scientist Bot (Magenta)** - Experimenting with fusion
3. **💥 Chaos Bot (Red)** - Creating random mayhem!

### **Quick Tips:**
- **Watch and learn** from their strategies

- **First player** to connect hosts the AI

## 💬 USING CHAT

### **Where Is It?**
- **Bottom-right corner** of the screen
- Green glowing window with **"💬 Chat"** header

### **How to Chat:**
1. Type message in the input box
2. Press **ENTER** or click **"Send"**
3. Everyone sees your message instantly!
4. Your name appears in **your ship's color**

### **Chat Controls:**
- **−** button = Minimize chat
- **+** button = Restore chat
- **Scroll** inside window to see history
- **Auto-scrolls** to new messages

---

## 🎮 QUICK CONTROLS REMINDER

### **Guns (Keys 1-0):**
- **1** = Proton Stringer
- **2** = Neutron Stringer  
- **3** = Electron Stringer
- **5** = Gluon Web (for fusion!)
- **8** = Anti-Stringer (creates chaos!)

### **Ship Movement:**
- **A** = Move left
- **D** = Move right
- **S** = Toggle gravity
- **Click & Drag** = Move ship anywhere
- **Click & Hold** = Continuous fire

### **Other:**
- **SHIFT + Scroll** = Change stage (0=Strings, 1=Atoms, 2=Molecules)
- **CTRL + Scroll** = Zoom level
- **Right Click** = Gun options

## 🆘 QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING

### **AI Bots Not Showing?**
- ✅ Make sure multiplayer is connected
- ✅ You need to be the **first player** (bots auto-spawn for host)
- ✅ Wait a few seconds after connecting
- ✅ Check right panel for "AI Players" status

### **Chat Not Appearing?**
- ✅ Connect to multiplayer first
- ✅ Look in **bottom-right corner**
- ✅ Try refreshing the page
- ✅ Check browser console for errors (F12)

### **Can't Send Messages?**
- ✅ Make sure you're connected to multiplayer
- ✅ Type something in the input box first!
- ✅ Press ENTER or click "Send"
- ✅ Check your internet connection
